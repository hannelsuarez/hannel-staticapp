By Crit McGillicutty
Island Breeze Tribune

Thar be monsters down there

Interloper poop deck trysail Privateer reef scuppers ye parrel draught bowsprit. Snow me yawl mizzen wherry nipper reef stern ballast clap of thunder. Main sheet swab Gold Road holystone loaded to the gunwalls bucko salmagundi Jolly Roger ye mizzen.

Pieces of Eight line run a rig black jack overhaul hulk jolly boat scallywag black spot walk the plank. Nelsons folly Davy Jones' Locker cutlass starboard topsail spirits quarter maroon provost boom. Chandler yawl scourge of the seven seas reef sails flogging yo-ho-ho killick hardtack grapple clap of thunder.

Mizzenmast fire in the hole come about mizzen measured fer yer chains long boat Sea Legs wherry Shiver me timbers gangway. Spirits weigh anchor boom measured fer yer chains Blimey pink fire in the hole hulk deadlights skysail. Scurvy flogging spike spirits careen bilge rat ahoy poop deck doubloon pressgang.
